Opportunity Lost

You step back from the neon portal, shaking your head. As the gateway fades, a nearby street vendor scoffs: "Ha! Another tourist too scared to see real future!"

Days later, news reports show Chongqing's spectacular Moon Festival - millions of drones creating breathtaking displays in the night sky. Your friends who went return with incredible stories of underground tech markets and rooftop tea houses overlooking the city of tomorrow.

"Coward! The future isn't for the faint of heart!"

Paradise Denied

You turn away from the tropical glow. The portal vanishes with a sound like a sighing wave. A travel agent nearby shakes their head: "Bali? I went last year. Changed my life. But I guess some people prefer staying in their comfort zones."

Months later, you meet a friend who visited Ubud. Their photos of jungle waterfalls and spiritual ceremonies at dawn make your heart ache. They speak of Balinese healers who solved chronic pain Western doctors couldn't touch.

"Your loss! The island gods won't call twice!"

Fiesta Missed

You wave away the vibrant portal. The mariachi music cuts off abruptly. An old man at a nearby bench chuckles: "Ay, gringo... you don't know what you just turned down. My village has fiesta tonight - best mole in Oaxaca, and my granddaughter's quinceañera!"

Later, you see social media posts from acquaintances who went - photos of them learning to make tortillas by hand, swimming in cenotes, dancing until sunrise. Their joy is palpable even through the screen.

"¡Qué lástima! You'll never know true fiesta now!"

Stories Untold

You shake your head at the cyclo driver. The portal flickers out like a dying motorbike engine. An elderly woman selling lotus flowers tuts: "My nephew came back from Saigon last week. Found his father's diary from the war... finally understood our family."

Years later, you read a novel set in Ho Chi Minh City - the descriptions of morning phở vendors and hidden courtyard cafes are so vivid you can almost taste the fish sauce. The author thanks the city for inspiring their best work.

"Foolish! The city's stories deserved to be heard!"
